Scene 2 with features highlighted
  1. Caustic lighting effects from light being focused by water.
  2. Soft shadows resulting from global illumination effects (light bouncing off of surfaces)
  3. Area light used to illuminate the scene
  4. Fresnel reflections of light off of the surface of the water creating caustic patterns
  5. Glossy highlights on the walls give the walls a more ceramic tile feel
  6. Normal mapping textures are used to give the illusion of depth in the grout lines between tiles on an otherwise flat geometric mesh
  7. Roughness textures are used in addition to color textures to make the paint stand out from the tiles
